The state of the world

I love many things about the world we live in, one being people. The world is full of them. The people in my life are nothing short of amazing.

I don't hate much in this world except intolerance.

I wouldn't change a thing about the world. Anne Frank's quote, "Despite everything, I belive that people are really good at heart." sums up my view on the world. People and things are innately good. But sometimes mistakes must be made to create progress.
