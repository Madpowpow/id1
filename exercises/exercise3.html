MGMT Website Analysis

  1. Describe the fonts used as headers, paragraphs, and pull quotes.

    2. For pull quotes and headers they use a scrawled handwriting which reminds me of electricity which goes along so well with the bands music, for the body they use a serif font that is easy to read against the crazy background.

  2. Describe the color palette. What colors are used? Where and how are specific colors used?

    3. The color pallet is all pastels, what reminds me of Easter colors. The colors are pastel blue, pink, yellow, and green. They are used mostly in the wobbling background.

  3. What role do the visuals play? When and how are visuals used?

    4. The visuals are hilarious and really exhibit the bands fun and eccentric vibe.

  4. How are sections separated? What are the visual cues?

    5. Sections are separated by the rolling visuals and pull quotes and bold san-serif typeface.
